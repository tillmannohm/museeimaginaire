Livestreams of popular artworks collectively reproduced by social media users.

Each artwork stream channels its latest representations from public Instagram posts based on their physical locations using geotags. Aided by computer vision, Le #museeimaginaire gathers shared auratic moments by museum visitors around the world in real-time.


Note: Since November 2018, the realtime feature of this work will only be available for exhibitions. To save resources and costs, this web presence shows the latest updated version.
